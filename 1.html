Human Positioning Prediction

The user's positioning can be predicted based on data obtained from the user's wearable devices. By training the machine learning models (random forest, support vector machines, neural network) with the data obtained from other users, the precision can achieve as high as 87% with the test dataset.

  • A: Lying, B: Sitting, C: Standing, D: Walking, E: Running, F: Cycling, G: Nordic Walking, H: Ascending stairs
  • I: Descending stairs, J: Vacuum cleaning, K: Ironing
